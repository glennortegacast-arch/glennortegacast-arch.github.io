From an employee party to a government-level inauguration.

I produced two very different corporate events at the plant: an internal Father's Day celebration for employees, and the official inauguration of the first hydrogen plant in the Americas — a high-profile event attended by the state governor and members of his cabinet.

The same production discipline, scaled from a relaxed internal celebration to a high-stakes institutional event.

01 Father's Day Celebration · Casino Theme

An evening off the clock.

An HR-driven initiative giving employees a space to relax, have fun and celebrate — deliberately different from the workday.

What I did

I led vendor sourcing and budget management end to end — coordinating casino tables, catering, gifts and decoration. I built two differentiated budget proposals (an economy and a premium option) for managerial approval, then iterated on scope and cost as the event's needs evolved until we landed on the right fit.

02 Hydrogen Plant Inauguration · Flagship

First hydrogen plant in the Americas.

Officially inaugurate Gerresheimer Querétaro's new hydrogen plant — built with supplier Cryoinfra — and host the state governor and his cabinet.

What I did

I coordinated event logistics in close partnership with government staff — joint planning and reviews from start to finish. I sourced and quoted the LED screen, designed the visual artwork projected on stage, managed on-site logistics and covered the event itself. I also coordinated with official media to secure press coverage in local newspapers.

Scope
Government coordinationLED screen sourcingStage artworkOn-site logisticsPress & media
The challenge

The entire program had to run within the strict time window of the governor's official agenda — zero margin for delay. The event ran quickly and smoothly, exactly on schedule.
