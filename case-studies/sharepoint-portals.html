01Context

Internal information without a home to live in.

Teams across two regions needed a single, reliable place for internal information — news, resources, quick links and shared tools. Without a structured hub, that content lived scattered across inboxes, drives and ad-hoc pages, different in every region.

The opportunity: give each region a portal that feels like one company, not a patchwork.

02My Role

From requirements to a functional, branded portal.

I owned the digital production end to end: gathering what each region actually needed, shaping the information architecture, and designing and building the portals directly in SharePoint — on-brand, navigable and ready for teams to use day to day.

One shared design language, adapted to two regional realities — so North America and Mexico read as the same system while serving their own audiences.
