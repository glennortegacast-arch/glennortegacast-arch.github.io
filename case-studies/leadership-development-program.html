01Context

Building the next generation of leaders from within.

Gerresheimer Querétaro needed a structured way to build leadership from within and develop its internal talent pipeline. The program targets employees across the plant who have been identified as future leaders.

A clear, deliberate path to grow into leadership roles — not left to chance.
