01Context

Repetitive work that quietly drained the team.

Across the plant, several high-volume, repetitive processes still ran manually — from tracking training-compliance documents for new hires to screening and scheduling blue-collar recruitment candidates one by one.

Each was slow, error-prone, and a recurring drain on the team's time.

I identified these as automation opportunities and built solutions to remove the manual overhead — turning recurring effort into systems that run themselves.

02What I built
Power Automate

Compliance reminders

Every new hire completes a 15-day training period, after which their Cardex document is due. I built a flow that runs the whole chase automatically — no manual intervention.

  • Reads from a central Excel tracker
  • Filters records by supervisor and counts outstanding documents
  • Sends a personalized reminder email in custom on-brand HTML
  • Runs on an automated schedule

ManyChat

Talent acquisition bot

For continuous blue-collar hiring, I designed an automated conversational flow that hands recruiters pre-filtered, ready-to-meet candidates instead of raw leads.

  • Vacancies on Facebook & WhatsApp route candidates into a chat
  • Pre-screens applicants through qualifying questions
  • Auto-schedules qualified candidates for an in-person plant visit
  • Delivers recruiters a ready-to-meet shortlist
