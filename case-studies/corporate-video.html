01Context

One plant, many stories — told on screen.

I produced a body of corporate video content for the plant: internal informational videos and organizational pieces covering health, safety, and production across different manufacturing lines.

Made for two audiences — employees and plant visitors — including one piece produced specifically to showcase the operation to a major client.

02My Role

The full production cycle — owned end to end.

I owned direction, cinematography and editing. Depending on the project, I worked solo, coordinated external vendors, or collaborated with the internal communications team to bring each video to completion.
